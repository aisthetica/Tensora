Oct 9, 12 PM Problem Release

Problem Statements Released

Tensora 2K25

Problem statements across 5 themes (Education, Transport, Healthcare, Agriculture, Sustainable Energy) will be released 6 hours before the hackathon begins. Teams can study and choose their preferred problem statement.

Oct 9, 6 PM - Oct 10, 6 PM 24-Hour Hackathon

Innovation Marathon Begins

Tensora 2K25

The 24-hour hackathon begins at 6 PM on October 9th and ends at 6 PM on October 10th. Teams must develop AI-powered solutions with mandatory implementation of ML/DL/AI techniques. All submissions must be completed by the deadline.

Oct 11 Evaluation & Results

Final Presentations & Judging

Tensora 2K25

Teams will present their projects to expert juries on October 11th. Each team gets 15 minutes for presentation including Q&A. Projects will be evaluated based on innovation, technical implementation, theme relevance, and presentation quality. Winners will be announced the same day.

Rules

Answers to the most frequently asked questions!

Who can participate?

The hackathon is open for students passionate about AI and innovation. It's perfect for anyone interested in learning and solving real-world problems with technology.

01

What's the team size?

Teams must consist of 2-4 members. We encourage diverse teams with complementary skills in AI, development, and design for the best results.

02

How to register?

Click the register button and fill out the registration form with your team details. Make sure to register before the deadline!

03

Is AI implementation mandatory?

Yes! All projects must include AI implementation using ML, DL, LLM, Computer Vision, NLP, or other AI techniques. This is what makes Tensora unique.

04

What are the submission requirements?

Teams must submit GitHub repo, demo video, presentation, and brief report. All submissions must be completed by 6 PM on Oct 10th.

05

What if I'm new to AI?

Don't worry! Passion for learning is more important than current experience. We encourage participation from all skill levels and provide guidance during the event.
