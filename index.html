About Tensora 2K25

Tensora 2K25 is a 24-hour AI & Innovation hackathon organized by the Department of Artificial Intelligence & Data Science. It's an intensive innovation marathon where young minds come together to solve real-world challenges through cutting-edge AI technology.

  • Date: 8th October to 10th October 2025
  • Mode: 8th–9th October Online, 10th October Offline at KLNCE
  • Team Size: 2 to 4 members
  • 24-hour innovation marathon
  • AI implementation mandatory
  • Cash prizes and certificates

Problem Statements

Choose from 5 themes with 4 challenging problem statements each

🚗

Transport

  • 1 AI-Based Predictive Maintenance of Metro Compressor Units
    Problem: The compressors in metro trains are critical components, and their unexpected failure can cause significant service disruptions, costly repairs, and potential safety risks. Manual or time-based maintenance often leads to either premature component replacement or a complete breakdown.

    Solution: Develop an AI-driven predictive maintenance system using multivariate sensor data (e.g., pressure, temperature, vibration) from compressor units. The model will learn normal operational patterns and predict the Remaining Useful Life (RUL) of components, enabling maintenance to be scheduled proactively before a failure occurs.
  • 2 Car Driver Distraction AI
    Problem: Driver distraction—caused by mobile phone use, drowsiness, or inattention—is one of the leading causes of road accidents worldwide. Current monitoring systems either fail to detect subtle distractions or react too late, increasing the risk of collisions and fatalities.

    Solution: Develop an AI-powered system that uses computer vision and deep learning to analyze the driver's facial expressions, eye movements, and head position through an in-car camera. The model will identify signs of distraction or drowsiness and issue real-time alerts or corrective actions.
  • 3 Flight Schedule Optimization at Congested Airports
    Problem: Heavily congested airports like Mumbai and Delhi frequently experience delays due to inefficient scheduling, runway constraints, and cascading disruptions from a single delayed flight. This impacts airlines, passengers, and overall operational efficiency.

    Solution: Build an AI model to optimize flight schedules based on historical flight data. The model will analyze peak-time delays and runway capacity to identify optimal takeoff/landing times and minimize disruptions, with an NLP interface for human decision-making.
  • 4 Human-Wildlife Conflict Mitigation on Railway Tracks
    Problem: Train-animal collisions, especially with elephants in forested areas of India, result in fatal accidents for wildlife, train damage, and trauma for railway staff. Current mitigation methods are often insufficient and reactive.

    Solution: Create an AI-based system that uses sensors and/or cameras along railway tracks to detect the presence of wildlife. The system will alert train operators in real-time, allowing them to slow down or stop the train and prevent a collision.

Education

  • 1 CodeAI – AI-Powered Interactive DSA Tutor
    Problem: Students struggle to grasp complex data structures and algorithms (DSA) concepts through static textbooks. They need a personalized, interactive, and visual learning tool that explains solutions and provides dynamic feedback.

    Solution: Develop an AI system that provides step-by-step textual solutions, interactive code visualizations, and a Q&A interface for DSA problems. The system will demonstrate how algorithms work on a given input and allow users to ask questions about specific steps or request alternative approaches.
  • 2 LangPairAI – AI for Contextual Language Translation
    Problem: Generic translation tools often fail to capture the nuances, idioms, and context-specific usage of a second language, which is a major barrier for language learners.

    Solution: Build an AI system that generates contextual translations, usage examples, and practice exercises for sentences and paragraphs. The system will provide feedback on errors, helping learners understand not just what a word means, but how it is used in different situations.
  • 3 GestureLearnAI – Vision-Based Sign & Gesture Tutoring
    Problem: Hearing-impaired students face significant communication barriers in mainstream classrooms, as there is a lack of real-time translation tools for sign language.

    Solution: Create an AI system that uses computer vision to recognize sign language gestures in real time and translate them into spoken or written text for teachers and peers. Conversely, it will translate teacher speech into animated signs or subtitles, enabling seamless two-way communication.
  • 4 AI-Powered OMR Sheet Evaluation System
    Problem: Manually checking filled OMR (Optical Mark Recognition) sheets is a time-consuming and error-prone process. Teachers and students need a fast and accurate way to evaluate these sheets and get instant feedback.

    Solution: Develop a mobile-based AI system that can instantly evaluate a filled OMR sheet from a smartphone photo. The system will automatically detect and align the sheet, identify filled bubbles, calculate marks against a provided answer key, and provide real-time analysis such as per-section performance.

Agriculture

  • 1 AI-Powered Crop Disease Detection
    Problem: Crop diseases are a major cause of reduced yield and financial loss for farmers. Manual inspection is slow and often happens too late to prevent the disease from spreading.

    Solution: Develop an AI system that can accurately detect and classify crop diseases from images of leaves, stems, or fruits taken with a smartphone or drone. The system will also estimate disease severity and provide recommendations for treatment or prevention.
  • 2 AI-Powered Soil Health Analysis & Crop Recommendation
    Problem: Farmers often lack precise data-driven insights into their soil's health, leading to inefficient crop selection and fertilizer use. Poor soil conditions can drastically reduce yield.

    Solution: Build an AI system that analyzes soil data (from sensors, images, or lab results) to assess its health (e.g., nutrients, pH). The system will then recommend the most suitable crops for optimal yield and suggest soil improvement measures.
  • 3 DairyAI – Milk Quality & Yield Prediction
    Problem: Dairy farmers need to efficiently monitor milk production and quality to ensure profitability and animal health. Unexpected drops in yield or quality can indicate underlying health issues.

    Solution: Create an AI system that predicts daily milk yield and detects quality issues based on data about cow health, feed intake, and environmental factors. The model will provide alerts for potential health issues that could affect production.
  • 4 AI-Powered Weed Mapping Using Satellite Images
    Problem: Weeds compete with crops for resources, significantly reducing yield. Mapping weed-infested areas across large farmlands is difficult and labor-intensive with traditional methods.

    Solution: Develop an AI system that analyzes satellite images of farmland to detect and map the distribution of weeds. This enables farmers to perform precision weed management, such as targeted herbicide application, which saves costs and reduces environmental impact.

Healthcare

  • 1 Prescription Risk Analysis System
    Problem: The risk of drug-drug interactions and dosage errors is a significant concern in healthcare, which can lead to adverse patient outcomes and medical complications.

    Solution: Develop an AI-powered system that parses textual prescription inputs to identify medications and dosages. The system will then leverage medical datasets to detect potential interactions and contraindications, assigning a severity score and providing a detailed analysis report for medical review.
  • 2 Intelligent Radiology Anomaly Detection
    Problem: Radiologists are often overloaded with a large volume of medical images (X-rays, CT scans, MRIs). Manually analyzing each image for subtle anomalies is time-consuming and can lead to missed diagnoses.

    Solution: Create an AI-based solution that analyzes medical images to automatically detect and classify anomalies. The system will localize abnormalities and generate a structured output indicating the anomaly type and its location, acting as a supportive tool for radiologists to prioritize critical cases.
  • 3 NutritionAI – Personalized Diet Recommendation
    Problem: Diet recommendations are often generic and do not account for an individual's unique health metrics, lifestyle, and medical conditions, which can be ineffective or even harmful.

    Solution: Build an AI system that takes a user's health data and lifestyle factors as input to recommend personalized diet plans. The system will generate tailored nutrient intake suggestions and provide explanations for each recommendation, helping users make informed dietary choices.
  • 4 AI-Based Early Detection of Parkinson's Disease
    Problem: Parkinson's Disease (PD) is a progressive neurodegenerative disorder, and early detection is critical for effective management. Traditional diagnosis can be subjective and time-consuming.

    Solution: Develop an AI system that can detect early signs of Parkinson's Disease from accessible data sources such as voice recordings (speech analysis), handwriting samples (micrographia detection), or motion patterns from wearable sensors. This non-invasive system can assist in timely diagnosis and treatment.

Sustainable Energy & Safety

  • 1 Renewable Energy Forecasting
    Problem: The variable nature of renewable energy sources like solar and wind makes it difficult to manage and integrate them into the power grid. Accurate forecasting is essential for grid stability and optimizing energy distribution.

    Solution: Build an AI model that predicts solar or wind energy generation. The model will use real-time weather data (e.g., cloud cover, wind speed) and geographic information to provide accurate and timely forecasts, helping grid operators better manage the supply and demand of renewable energy.
  • 2 Industrial Carbon Emissions Tracking & Optimization
    Problem: Industries are under increasing pressure to reduce their carbon footprint, but they often lack a comprehensive way to track and measure emissions across their entire operation. This makes it difficult to identify emission hotspots and set effective reduction targets.

    Solution: Create an integrated digital solution that tracks, calculates, and visualizes carbon emissions in real time. The system will analyze data from raw material sourcing, production, and logistics to identify emission hotspots, automate compliance reporting, and provide actionable insights for achieving carbon reduction goals.
  • 3 AI-Based Worker Safety Monitoring
    Problem: Industrial and construction sites are high-risk environments where manual safety supervision is often insufficient and cannot prevent all accidents from unsafe behavior or environmental hazards.

    Solution: Develop an AI system that uses cameras and sensors to monitor worker safety in real time. The system will detect unsafe behaviors (e.g., not wearing a helmet), identify environmental hazards (e.g., gas leaks), and trigger immediate alerts to prevent accidents.
  • 4 GridWise – Smart Energy Consumption Optimizer
    Problem: Households and industries often consume energy inefficiently during peak hours, which leads to higher electricity costs and places a strain on the power grid.

    Solution: Create an AI tool that analyzes energy consumption patterns and recommends optimal usage schedules to reduce peak loads and costs. The system will identify peak consumption periods and suggest load-shifting strategies, such as running appliances at off-peak hours, to help users save money and promote a more stable grid.

Rules

Answers to the most frequently asked questions!

Who can participate?

The hackathon is open for students passionate about AI and innovation. It's perfect for anyone interested in learning and solving real-world problems with technology.

01

What's the team size?

Teams must consist of 2-4 members. We encourage diverse teams with complementary skills in AI, development, and design for the best results.

02

How to register?

Click the register button and fill out the registration form with your team details. Make sure to register before the deadline!

03

Is AI implementation mandatory?

Yes! All projects must include AI implementation using ML, DL, LLM, Computer Vision, NLP, or other AI techniques. This is what makes Tensora unique.

04

What are the submission requirements?

Teams must submit GitHub repo, demo video, presentation, and brief report. All submissions must be completed by 6 PM on Oct 9th.

05

What if I'm new to AI?

Don't worry! Passion for learning is more important than current experience. We encourage participation from all skill levels and provide guidance during the event.
